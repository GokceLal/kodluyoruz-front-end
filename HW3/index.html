Recipe

Ingredients for cake

  • 200g butter
  • 250g sugar
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract
  • 300g flour
  • 300ml milk
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp baking soda
  • 1/2 tsp salt

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 180 C
  2. Beat butter and sugar together in a large bowl
  3. Mix in vanilla extract and flour
  4. Mix in milk and baking powder
  5. Add baking soda and salt
  6. Beat in milk until smooth
  7. Heat oven to 180 C
  8. Brush with flour and bake for 10-15 minutes
